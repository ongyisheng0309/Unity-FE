## :dart: Description ##

FireEx VR is a state-of-the-art virtual reality (VR) application developed to provide users with immersive training experiences in the handling of various types of fire extinguishers. By simulating real fires in different environments, FireEx VR offers a hands-on approach to mastering the proper techniques for effectively extinguishing fires. With its interactive interface and detailed simulations, it is designed to equip individuals with the skills and confidence to respond quickly and effectively to fire emergencies.

## :sparkles: Features ##

- **Hands-On Fire Extinguisher Training:** Users can engage in realistic fire extinguisher simulations, practicing the correct techniques for different types of fires.
- **Customizable Fire Scenarios:** Adjust the intensity and type of fire, as well as environmental factors like wind and smoke, to simulate diverse emergency situations.
- **Real-Time Feedback:** FireEx VR provides immediate feedback on extinguisher usage, highlighting areas for improvement and reinforcing correct actions.
- **Progress Tracking:** Keep track of your training progress and proficiency levels in extinguishing various types of fires.


## :rocket: Installation ##

1. Clone the Repository to Your Local Machine: ```git clone https://github.com/Oasis-Uniandes/Fire-Extinguisher-Training```
2. Install the Oculus App and configure your headset: <a href="https://www.oculus.com/download_app/?id=1582076955407037" target="_blank"> Meta Quest 2 Software </a>
4. Go to the Steam store and download SteamVR:  <a href="https://store.steampowered.com/app/250820/SteamVR/" target="_blank"> Steam VR Software </a>
5. After installations, open the project in Unity.
